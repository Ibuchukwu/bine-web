Why Bine?

Bine was born out of the stress students and course reps face when managing bulk payments. With Bine, every payment is recorded securely, accurately, and without stress. You can say goodbye to missing names, wrong sums, or tiring cross-checks.

Whether you’re paying your textbook dues, registering a course, or tracking who paid what, Bine makes it fast, secure, and paper-free.
